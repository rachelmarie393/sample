My Favorite Foods

Growing up in New York, my favorite restaurant was the Akron House. They had carrot cake, which was my favorite dessert. Here are my favorite desserts that I enjoyed at the Akron House:

  1. Carrot Cake
  2. Cheesecake
  3. Chocolate Gelato

Do I remember any of the mains from the many times I ate there as a child? Not at all. You can tell where my priorities lay at the time!
